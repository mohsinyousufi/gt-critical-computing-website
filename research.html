ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Neural Network Optimization

Developing next-generation neural architectures that can learn more efficiently with less data. Focus on transformer models and attention mechanisms.


Explainable AI

Creating AI systems that can provide clear explanations for their decisions, critical for high-stakes applications in healthcare and finance.


AI Ethics & Fairness

Researching methods to ensure AI systems are fair, unbiased, and aligned with human values across diverse populations.

QUANTUM COMPUTING

Quantum Algorithms

Developing novel quantum algorithms for optimization problems, cryptography, and machine learning applications.


Error Correction

Working on advanced quantum error correction codes to make quantum computers more reliable and practical for real-world use.


Quantum Cryptography

Designing quantum-safe encryption methods and exploring quantum key distribution protocols for secure communications.

CYBERSECURITY

Advanced Threat Detection

Using machine learning and behavioral analysis to detect sophisticated cyberattacks in real-time across network infrastructures.


Zero-Trust Architecture

Developing comprehensive zero-trust security frameworks for modern distributed computing environments.


Post-Quantum Cryptography

Creating encryption methods that will remain secure even against future quantum computer attacks.
